Pallet Rack

Pallet rack is a material handling storage system designed to store materials on pallets . Although there are many varieties of pallet racking, all types allow for the storage of palletized materials in horizontal rows with multiple levels. Forklift trucks are usually required to place the loaded pallets onto the racks for storage. Pallet racks are used throughout industry and distribution facilities for bulk storage of items.


Decking is commonly used as a safety measure on pallet rack to prevent pallets or the products stored on them from falling through the rack structure. Decking comes in various thicknesses according to your load requirement.
